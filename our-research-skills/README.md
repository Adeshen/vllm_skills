# Our Research Skills

This directory contains local research-support skills for paper discovery, reading maps, and evidence synthesis.

| Skill | Purpose |
| --- | --- |
| [paper-skill](paper-skill/SKILL.md) | Search related papers from seed literature, build reading maps, and connect papers to vLLM/MLSys bug, fuzzing, incident, CI, and serving themes. |

## Boundary

- Research skills help find and summarize literature.
- They do not prove local vLLM or vLLM-Ascend behavior.
- Paper facts should be cited to primary sources such as arXiv, venue pages, publisher pages, or project repositories.
